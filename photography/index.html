I started photographing things when I was just 9 years old, thanks to my first smartphone. It was super cheap and not the best in quality, but it held a special place in my heart because my mum bought it for me.

In 2023, I had the amazing opportunity to play with one of my friend's DSLR cameras. That day, I learned how to use a DSLR, and the difference in quality was astonishing compared to my early smartphone photos! It was a fun and eye-opening experience that reignited my passion for photography, making me excited to capture the world with better tools.
